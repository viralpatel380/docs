## Authentication Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Auth Page">
    Open your web browser and navigate to
    [https://lisaapp.in/auth](https://lisaapp.in/auth).
    <Info>Bookmark this page for quick access in the future.</Info>
     <Frame caption="LISA Authentication Page">
          <img src="/images/login.png" />
        </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Input your registered email address and password in the respective fields.
    <Warning>
      Ensure caps lock is off and you're using the correct email associated with
      your LISA account.
    </Warning>
     
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Organization">
    After entering your credentials, you'll be prompted to select your
    organization from a dropdown menu.
    <Tip>
      If you're part of multiple organizations, make sure to choose the correct
      one for your current session.
    </Tip>
    <Frame caption="Choose Organization">
          <img src="/images/orgs.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Choose Platfrom">
    After selecting organization, you'll be prompted to select platform. If you have access for both the platform.
    <Note>
     If you have access to both the platform, you can choose the platform you want to access. But if you have only one platform access, you will be redirected to that platform directly.
    </Note>
    <Frame caption="Choose Platforms LXP or MLP">
          <img src="/images/platforms.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Role-based Redirection">
    Based on your role, the system will automatically redirect you to the
    appropriate interface:
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Learner">
        Access to Learning Experience Platofrm (LXP) as learner at <code>xyz.lisaapp.in</code>
        <Frame caption="Learning Experience Platform ( lisa LXP )">
          <img src="/images/learner.png" />
        </Frame>
      Micro Learning Platform ( lisa ai ) at <code>xyz.lisaapp.in/lisa-ai</code>
         <Frame caption="Micro Learning Platform ( lisa AI )">
          <img src="/images/lisaai.png" />
        </Frame>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Admin">
       Access to Admin Console as admin role and other custom roles. <code>xyz.lisaapp.in/admin</code>
        <Frame caption="Admin Console for LXP and MLP">
          <img src="/images/admin.png" />
        </Frame>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Trainer">
        Access to Trainer Console as trainer for conducting sessions and assignment checking. <code>xyz.lisaapp.in/trainer</code>
        <Frame caption="Trainer Console for LXP">
          <img src="/images/trainer.png" />
        </Frame>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Having trouble logging in? Check our [troubleshooting
  guide](/troubleshooting/login-issues) or contact your organization's LISA
  administrator.
</Tip>
